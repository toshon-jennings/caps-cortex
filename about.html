CAps™ Creator

CAps™ is a portable, trust-forward correction utility designed to complement autocorrect and streamline text revision workflows. It operates as a lightweight enhancement layer, giving users rapid access to trusted corrections wherever keystrokes are happening—engineered for speed, accuracy, and user empowerment.

The CAps cockpit, "CAps-Cortex™," showcases technical implementation, visual design, documentation polish, and UX focus—essentially, the nuts-and-bolts of my product stewardship philosophy focused on clarity, trust, and respect for the user.
